Why Mount Kilimanjaro?

A guide for future trekkers — routes, preparation, and cultural context.

When to go

Best months: January–March and June–October for clearer weather.

Routes

  • Marangu — classic route
  • Machame — scenic & varied
  • Lemosho — gradual and panoramic

Packing tips

  • Layered clothing
  • Warm sleeping bag
  • Walking poles and sturdy boots
