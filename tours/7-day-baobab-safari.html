7-Day Baobab Safari & Cultural Immersion

Overview: A crafted journey through Serengeti plains and local communities where baobab trees anchor stories and shelter traditions.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Arrival to Arusha, welcome briefing and overnight.
  2. Day 2 — Transfer to Tarangire, afternoon game drives beneath baobabs.
  3. Day 3 — Serengeti transfer and evening game drive.
  4. Day 4 — Full day in Serengeti; optional balloon safari (extra cost).
  5. Day 5 — Village cultural visit and homestay experience near baobab groves.
  6. Day 6 — Ngorongoro crater tour; wildlife-rich crater floor.
  7. Day 7 — Return to Arusha and departure.

Inclusions

  • Professional local guide and 4x4 vehicle
  • Park fees (where indicated)
  • Accommodation and meals as specified

Exclusions

  • International flights
  • Optional activities (balloon safari), gratuities, visas
