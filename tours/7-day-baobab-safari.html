Tour Overview

Our signature 7-Day Baobab Safari is designed to showcase Tanzania's most iconic wildlife destinations while highlighting the magnificent baobab trees that have become our namesake. This comprehensive journey takes you through four of Tanzania's premier national parks, each offering unique landscapes and wildlife viewing opportunities.

From the endless plains of the Serengeti to the dramatic Ngorongoro Crater, from the elephant-rich Tarangire to the bird-filled Lake Manyara, this safari offers the perfect introduction to Tanzania's incredible biodiversity. You'll witness the Big Five, encounter ancient baobab trees, and experience the raw beauty of Africa's wilderness.

Highlights

  • Witness the Great Migration in Serengeti National Park
  • Explore the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater
  • Photograph massive elephant herds in Tarangire
  • Spot tree-climbing lions in Lake Manyara
  • Marvel at ancient baobab trees up to 3,000 years old
  • Experience diverse ecosystems and landscapes
  • Enjoy comfortable accommodations and expert guiding

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Arusha

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you'll be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel in Arusha. After check-in, enjoy a welcome briefing about your safari adventure. Spend the evening at leisure, preparing for your journey ahead.

Accommodation: Arusha Hotel
Meals: Dinner
Activities: Airport transfer, welcome briefing
Day 2

Arusha to Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park, known as Tanzania's elephant capital. The park is famous for its massive elephant herds and ancient baobab trees. Enjoy a full day game drive, spotting elephants, lions, leopards, and over 500 bird species.

Accommodation: Tarangire Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Full day game drive in Tarangire
Day 3

Tarangire to Serengeti National Park

Early morning game drive in Tarangire, then drive to Serengeti National Park via the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Enter the Serengeti and enjoy an afternoon game drive in search of the Big Five and the Great Migration herds.

Accommodation: Serengeti Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Morning game drive, transfer to Serengeti, afternoon game drive
Day 4

Full Day in Serengeti National Park

Spend a full day exploring the endless plains of the Serengeti. Search for the Great Migration, spot predators on the hunt, and enjoy a picnic lunch in the wilderness. The Serengeti offers some of the best wildlife viewing in Africa.

Accommodation: Serengeti Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Full day game drive in Serengeti
Day 5

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

Morning game drive in Serengeti, then drive to Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Descend into the crater for an afternoon game drive in this natural wonder, home to the highest density of wildlife in Africa.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Morning game drive, crater descent, afternoon game drive
Day 6

Ngorongoro to Lake Manyara

Early morning crater floor game drive, then drive to Lake Manyara National Park. This small but diverse park is famous for its tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and diverse birdlife. Enjoy a full day exploring this unique ecosystem.

Accommodation: Lake Manyara Hotel
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Crater game drive, transfer to Lake Manyara, afternoon game drive
Day 7

Lake Manyara to Arusha

Morning game drive in Lake Manyara, then drive back to Arusha. Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport for your departure flight, or extend your stay with additional activities.

Accommodation: None
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Activities: Morning game drive, transfer to airport

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I pack for this safari? ‚ñº

Pack neutral-colored clothing, comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, binoculars, camera, and warm layers for early morning game drives. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.

What's the best time to see the Great Migration? ‚ñº

The Great Migration is a year-round phenomenon, but the best viewing is typically December-May in the southern Serengeti and June-November in the northern Serengeti. Our guides will track the herds' location.

Are there any health requirements? ‚ñº

Yellow fever vaccination is required for entry to Tanzania. Malaria prophylaxis is recommended. Consult your doctor 4-6 weeks before travel for personalized health advice.

What's the accommodation like? ‚ñº

We use comfortable mid-range lodges and tented camps with private bathrooms, hot water, and modern amenities. All accommodations are carefully selected for their location and quality.
