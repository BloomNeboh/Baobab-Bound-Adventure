Meet Our Founder

Eliud Mchome

As the visionary behind Baobab Bound Adventures, Eliud Mchome has dedicated over 15 years to African wildlife conservation and tourism. Born and raised in Tanzania, Eliud's passion for his homeland's natural beauty and cultural heritage drives our mission to create meaningful, sustainable travel experiences.

Eliud holds a Master's degree in Wildlife Management and is certified by the Tanzania Tourist Board. He has led over 500 successful safaris and is a recognized expert in East African wildlife behavior and conservation practices.

Our Mission

At Baobab Bound Adventures, we believe that travel has the power to transform lives while protecting the natural world. Our mission is to create authentic, sustainable safari experiences that connect travelers with Africa's incredible wildlife and rich cultural heritage.

We are committed to:

  • Supporting local communities through responsible tourism
  • Contributing to wildlife conservation efforts
  • Providing exceptional, personalized service
  • Educating travelers about environmental protection
  • Creating meaningful cultural exchanges
